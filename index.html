Capabilities

Built to See Everything

01 —
Real-Time AI Detection

Continuously analyzes live camera footage to identify traffic lights, stop signs, pedestrians, vehicles, bicycles, and road hazards — frame by frame, instant by instant.

02 —
Audio Alerts

Spoken notifications powered by ElevenLabs voices — "Stop sign ahead", "Pedestrian detected" — letting you keep your eyes exactly where they belong: the road.

03 —
Traffic Light Intelligence

HSV color-space analysis determines red, yellow, or green states with precision beyond basic object detection — giving drivers actionable awareness, not just noise.

04 —
Bilingual: EN / ES

Road safety is for everyone. DriveAEye speaks English and Spanish — because life-saving technology shouldn't have a language barrier.

05 —
DriveStats Analytics

Every drive recorded, every object catalogued. Turn your daily commute into a data-rich awareness report that grows smarter over time.

Custom Alert Profiles

Choose exactly what you're alerted about. Pedestrians only. Signs only. Everything. Your road, your rules — zero alert fatigue.
