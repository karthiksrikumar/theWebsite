Simple to Use

How DriveAEye Works

No special equipment. No installation. Just your phone.

1
Mount your smartphone

Place your phone on your dashboard or windshield where the camera faces the road ahead — just like a dashcam.

2
Open DriveAEye

Launch the app, choose your language and alert preferences, and tap Start. That's it.

3
AI watches the road

The camera feeds live video to the AI, which identifies objects and road conditions in real time — every second you drive.

4
You hear clear alerts

"Stop sign ahead." "Pedestrian on the left." Calm, spoken warnings come through your speaker so you never have to glance at a screen.
